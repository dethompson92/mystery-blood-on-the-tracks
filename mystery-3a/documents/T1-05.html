II. Descripción de la Escena del Incidente

Al llegar a las 01:29 hrs, los agentes encontraron al sujeto previamente extraído del agua por personal de seguridad del hotel, tendido sobre la cubierta norte de la piscina. El personal de emergencias médicas ya se encontraba en el lugar intentando maniobras de reanimación. El sujeto fue declarado muerto a las 01:44 hrs.

En la cubierta de la piscina, en la esquina noreste, se localizaron una secadora de cabello y una extensión eléctrica de color naranja. La secadora se encontraba en posición de encendido (interruptor en ON). La extensión estaba desconectada del contacto al momento de la llegada de los agentes. El contacto GFCI ubicado detrás de la barra de la piscina se hallaba en estado de desactivación manual, confirmado por el personal de mantenimiento del hotel.

Se observaron quemaduras visibles en la mano y antebrazo izquierdo del sujeto fallecido, consistentes con quemaduras por descarga eléctrica, según observación del paramédico de turno, Técnico Eduardo Mora Segura.

II. Scene Description

Upon arrival at 01:29 AM, officers found the subject had already been extracted from the water by hotel security staff and was lying on the pool's north deck. Emergency medical personnel were already on scene attempting resuscitation. The subject was pronounced deceased at 01:44 AM.

Located on the pool deck, in the northeast corner, were one (1) hair dryer and one (1) orange extension cord. The hair dryer's power switch was in the ON position. The extension cord was disconnected from the outlet at the time of officer arrival. The GFCI safety outlet behind the pool bar was found in a manually disabled state, confirmed by hotel maintenance staff.

Visible burn marks on the left hand and left forearm of the deceased were observed by attending paramedic Eduardo Mora Segura, consistent with electrical contact injury.

Officer Note — Subinspector Estrada
The hair dryer and extension cord were immediately secured as potential evidence (items marked EV-001 and EV-002). The GFCI circuit bypass is significant: this circuit, if functioning, would have automatically cut power to the outlet within milliseconds of detecting ground fault (i.e., the dryer contacting the water). Its deliberate disabling indicates prior knowledge of the circuit's protective function. This is not consistent with an accident.

IV. Observaciones del Oficial Investigador

Con base en la inspección inicial de la escena y los testimonios preliminares del personal del hotel, el Subinspector Estrada concluye que la muerte del señor Solomon Vance no presenta características consistentes con accidente ni con suicidio. La desactivación deliberada del circuito de seguridad GFCI, la presencia de equipo de producción fuera del área de almacenamiento habitual y la interrupción no programada de las cámaras de vigilancia durante casi tres horas indican la planificación previa de un delito.

El caso ha sido clasificado provisionalmente como homicidio doloso bajo el artículo 302 del Código Penal Federal, y está sujeto a investigación por la FGE del Estado de Quintana Roo con apoyo de la SSPC. Se han notificado autoridades consulares de los Estados Unidos.

🇺🇸 English Translation
IV. Investigating Officer's Observations

Based on the initial scene inspection and preliminary statements from hotel staff, Subinspector Estrada concludes that the death of Solomon Vance is not consistent with accident or suicide. The deliberate disabling of the GFCI safety circuit, the presence of production equipment outside its designated storage, and the unscheduled interruption of surveillance cameras for nearly three hours are all consistent with premeditated criminal action.

The case has been provisionally classified as premeditated homicide (homicidio doloso) under Article 302 of the Federal Penal Code, and is under investigation by the FGE of Quintana Roo with SSPC support. United States Consular authorities have been notified.

Key Investigator Notes — For Investigators
1. The extension cord (EV-002) came from the production equipment room — not a guest room. Ask: who had access to that room?
2. The GFCI was manually disabled. This is not something that happens by accident. Someone who knew about electrical safety systems did this deliberately.
3. The cameras were offline for 2h 45m. This is far too long for a random malfunction, and it perfectly covers the estimated window of death.
4. Two drinks were at the pool. Did the victim meet someone there?
