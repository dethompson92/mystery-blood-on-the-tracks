The Killer: Amanda Foster

Title: VP Clinical Development

The Motive

Amanda had orchestrated a massive cover-up regarding the severe cardio-toxicity of the Phase II experimental compound. The pharmaceutical backing was worth $800M. Dr. Voss, ruthless as she was, refused to sign off on fraudulent data that would kill patients, and was preparing to blow the whistle to the FDA the following morning. Amanda needed her silenced immediately to protect the acquisition merge.

How It Happened

Amanda manipulated the access logs of James Wu (Lab Tech) to make it look like he checked out the lethal dose of the compound. She met Voss in her office under the guise of an emergency budget meeting, slipped the concentrated compound into Voss's habitual black coffee, and wiped her fingerprints from the mug. The overdose mimicked a massive stress-induced heart attack, consistent with the drug's hidden side effects.
