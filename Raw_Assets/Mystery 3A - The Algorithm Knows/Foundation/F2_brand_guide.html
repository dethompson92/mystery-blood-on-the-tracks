01 — Show Overview

The Forever Match Premise

"Twelve single contestants. An AI algorithm trained on seven years of compatibility data. One paradise resort. The algorithm doesn't guess — it knows. And when love is engineered, there's no room for error."

Forever Match is a luxury-reality competition series produced by Solara Entertainment Group and streamed on the NovaStar Network. Contestants are matched by a proprietary AI algorithm — "ARIA" (Adaptive Relationship Intelligence Architecture) — which analyzes behavioral data, biometric readings, and compatibility metrics gathered covertly throughout the production process.

Season 4 of the show is filmed over 28 days at the Gran Horizonte Resort & Spa in Cancún, Quintana Roo, Mexico. Twelve contestants compete for a $2 million prize and a guaranteed partnership validated by ARIA's matching score. The show's central tension: do the contestants trust the algorithm — or their hearts?

07 — Voice & Tone

Writing Style Guidelines

Forever Match Show Voice
Production documents, contestant bios, promotional copy
  • Aspirational, glossy, aspirationally modern
  • Emotional but controlled — "connection," "compatibility," "journey"
  • AI-forward: treat ARIA as authoritative, almost reverential
  • Subtle corporate euphemisms — never mention death or violence
  • Present tense for show copy; past tense only in police reports
  • Spanish marketing terms blended naturally (e.g., "paraíso," "destino")
  • Emoji/icon use permitted in social media archive docs only
Investigative Document Voice
Police reports, forensic files, legal documents
  • Clinical, objective, past tense throughout
  • Official case numbers and citations on every document
  • Bilingual: official body text in Spanish; English translation block below each section
  • IBM Plex Mono for all technical data, measurements, timestamps
  • No emotional language — describe observations only
  • Cross-reference other case documents by file number
  • Redaction bars [████] for information players cannot yet see
