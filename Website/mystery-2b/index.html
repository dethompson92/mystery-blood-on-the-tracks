BLOOD ON THE TRACKS

A 1957 cold case reopened. The original Sunset Limited steam train holds a deadly secret.

Case Overview

Case ID: NOPD-57-1144 / Reopened 2024
Location: The Sunset Limited (New Orleans to Los Angeles)
Date: October 1957

On October 23, 1957, prominent art dealer Vincent Marchand was found bludgeoned to death in his private sleeping car aboard the Sunset Limited, just outside El Paso, Texas.

The original investigation stalled amidst Cold War paranoia, vanished witnesses, and a seemingly impossible timeline. Now, with new forensic technology and recently declassified FBI files from 2024, the case has been reopened.

You must sift through two eras of evidence: the dusted-off 1957 witness statements, and the modern 2024 cold case laboratory analysis. Can you find what the detectives missed over sixty years ago?
