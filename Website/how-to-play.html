How to Play

A step-by-step guide to hosting and solving a Murder Mystery Collective case.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Setup Phase

One person acts as the Game Master (GM). The GM should review the GM Portal using the access code provided with the game materials. They will have access to the solution, tier passwords, and facilitation guides to help shape the narrative.

2. Player Roles

Players act as lead detectives on the scene. You can play cooperatively or competitively in small groups. Read the Foundation documents and Suspect Dossiers first to understand the world and those involved.

3. Tier Progression

Evidence is divided into Tiers. You begin with Tier 1. To unlock subsequent tiers, you must solve a puzzle, find an inconsistency, or discover a specific passcode within the current evidence. Entering this passcode unlocks the next tier.

4. Gathering Evidence

Review interview transcripts, forensic reports, camera logs, and physical items. Use the Evidence Board on each mystery's case page. Use the Gallery to zoom in on key visual details.

5. Accusation & Reveal

Once all evidence is reviewed, build your theory. You must establish Means, Motive, and Opportunity for one specific suspect. Present your accusation to the GM. They will then read the final Solution narrative.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I play solo?

Yes, absolutely. You can act as your own GM by only looking at the Solution document once you are confident you have solved the case.

Do I need to print anything?

No. The entire experience is fully hosted on this platform. However, physical evidence printing guides are provided in the GM portal if you prefer a tactile experience.

Are there any time limits?

There is no strict timer unless the GM decides to impose one. Take your time, cross-reference data, and enjoy the story.
