THE FINAL PRESCRIPTION

Ethics and ambition collide fatally in the cold corridors of clinical research.

Case Overview

Case ID: PORTLAND-PD-24-12-092
Location: Riverside Medical Research Institute, Portland, OR
Date: December 2024

Dr. Helena Voss, the ruthless but brilliant Chief Medical Officer at Riverside Medical Research Institute, was found dead in her locked office. Initial reports suggest an overdose of an experimental compound currently undergoing Phase II clinical trials.

But Dr. Voss was not suicidal. She was on the verge of either a Nobel Prize or a massive corporate scandal, depending on who you ask.

With millions of dollars in pharmaceutical grants on the line and careers hanging in the balance, several high-ranking doctors and researchers had both the means and the motive to silence her.

Victim: Dr. Helena Voss, CMO.
