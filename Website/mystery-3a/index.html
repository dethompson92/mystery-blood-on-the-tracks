THE ALGORITHM NEVER LIES

Solomon Vance is dead. The EROS algorithm matched him with his killer.

Case Overview

Case ID: FGE-QR-2025-07-0847
Location: Gran Horizonte Resort & Spa, Cancún, Mexico
Date: July 2025

The finale week of "Forever Match" on the Pulse Network was supposed to be the television event of the year. Instead, exactly at midnight, Solomon Vance—the universally reviled star of the reality dating show—was found dead in the rooftop infinity pool, the victim of deliberate electrocution.

Security sealed the building immediately. No one left. The killer is still inside among the contestants and crew.

You have been provided with the raw case files seized by the Fiscalia General del Estado. You will cross-reference alibis, comb through the production's internal communications, and analyze the EROS matchmaking algorithm itself to find the truth.
