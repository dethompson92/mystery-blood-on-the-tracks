Overview

Rafael Rios, 35, is an architect from Mexico City who runs a sustainable architecture practice with a focus on eco-conscious residential and commercial design. A UNAM (Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México) Architecture School graduate, he is widely regarded as a rising voice in Latin American sustainable design. He joined Forever Match at the suggestion of his publicist to gain exposure in the US market.

Of the six persons of interest, Rafael is the most guarded with investigators. He is measured, polite, and notably careful in his choice of words. His relationship with Solomon Vance is characterized by investigators as "unusual in nature" — the specific details of which Rafael has declined to fully elaborate on.

⚡ Relationship to Victim & Documented Conflict

Rafael acknowledges knowing Solomon Vance through normal show interactions. He states their relationship was "cordial and unremarkable." However, two crew members independently report observing a private conversation between Solomon and Rafael in the resort corridor on Day 16 that appeared "tense" and left Rafael visibly unsettled.

Rafael has not disclosed the subject of this conversation to investigators. When asked directly, he stated: "Solomon talked to a lot of people about a lot of things. I don't think our conversation is relevant to your investigation."
