Overview

Solomon Vance, 33, was a Miami-based technology entrepreneur and co-founder of Clarity Pay, a FinTech application acquired for $14 million in 2023. He was a registered contestant on Forever Match, Season 1, in the third week of filming at the Gran Horizonte Resort & Spa in Cancún, Mexico.

His body was discovered by resort security guard Sergio Jiménez at approximately 1:15 AM on July 19, 2025, in the North Tower rooftop infinity pool (Floor 15). Initial assessment by resort medical staff identified possible electrocution. FGE-QR investigators were notified at 1:30 AM. SEMEFO forensic examination is ongoing.

Solomon Vance built his reputation in Miami's technology and startup ecosystem. His FinTech app Clarity Pay was acquired for $14 million in 2023, positioning him as a rising figure in the early-stage investment community. He was known publicly as charming, sharp, and relentlessly networked. Colleagues described him as someone who "always knew something you didn't."

According to production records, Solomon applied for Forever Match through standard contestant intake in March 2025. His application cited personal reasons — "looking for a genuine connection outside the transactional world of tech." He was matched at 94.7% compatibility with fellow contestant Kavya Nair — the highest compatibility score of the season.

💔 Show Participation & Known Conflicts

By the time of his death, Solomon was completing Day 19 of a 42-day filming schedule. Despite being paired with Kavya Nair under the EROS compatibility algorithm, his behavior on the show generated significant interpersonal conflict. The following incidents are documented in production records and contestant witness accounts:

  • ⚡Public rejection of Kavya Nair (Day 12): During a filmed compatibility challenge, Solomon publicly declared he felt no romantic connection with his algorithmically assigned match, Kavya Nair. The interaction was described by witnesses as "deliberately humiliating." The clip circulated on social media before the episode aired.
  • ⚡Confrontation with Dominic Wallace (Day 18, 9:47 PM): Less than 3 hours before his death, contestant Dominic Wallace confronted Solomon poolside in front of witnesses and on camera, stating: "You're a snake. You play games with real people's feelings. You're dead to me — dead to me." The confrontation was captured on two production cameras.
  • ⚡Reports of unsanctioned contestant interactions: Multiple crew members noted that Solomon engaged in off-camera interactions with contestants not assigned as his match, in apparent violation of show protocols.
  • ⚡Unverified rumor circulation: An unverified allegation was circulating among contestants in the days before the murder concerning Solomon's role in spreading personal information about other cast members. Investigators are pursuing the details of this claim.

🔍 Scene & Preliminary Findings

Solomon Vance was found submerged in the shallow end of the Roof 15 infinity pool, face-up. An electrical appliance was recovered from the pool area. Resort medical staff who responded at 1:18 AM noted visible burn marks at the extremities consistent with electrical contact. Official cause of death is pending SEMEFO forensic examination.

The North Tower security camera system experienced a malfunction beginning at approximately 10:02 PM on July 18, which remained active until approximately 1:00 AM on July 19. A total of 38 cameras were affected. FGE-QR investigators are treating the malfunction as potentially significant to the investigation.

Personal effects recovered from Solomon's person at scene: one room keycard (Room 1201), one Apple Watch (screen cracked), no mobile phone. Solomon's mobile phone has not yet been located.
