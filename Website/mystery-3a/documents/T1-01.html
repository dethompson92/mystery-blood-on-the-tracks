But someone on that production had a secret worth killing for. And they knew exactly how to use the show's own technology against itself.

The police have opened a formal investigation under Case File FGE-QR-2025-07-0847. You have been granted access to a selection of official documents, production records, and witness accounts. It is now your job to piece together what happened — and why.

Before You Begin

Investigator Guidelines

  • 🔍
    Read everything. The answer is in the documents. Details that seem insignificant often matter.
  • 📝
    Take notes. Cross-reference names, times, and locations across documents. Inconsistencies are clues.
  • ⏱️
    Pay attention to timelines. When did things happen? Who was where? Are any accounts contradictory?
