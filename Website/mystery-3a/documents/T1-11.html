Overview

Zara Mirza, 30, is a second-year OB/GYN resident at Northwestern Memorial Hospital in Chicago, currently on a six-week leave of absence for filming. She holds a medical degree from Johns Hopkins School of Medicine and is widely described by fellow contestants as "the most composed person in the house."

Zara is algorithmically matched with Dominic Wallace. She is a person of interest because of documented conflict with Solomon Vance in the week preceding his death — specifically, a confrontation following the spread of a false and medically damaging rumor attributed to Solomon.

⚡ Relationship to Victim & Documented Conflict

Zara and Solomon had a documented interaction that escalated into direct confrontation approximately five days before the murder. According to multiple crew members, a rumor began circulating among the cast that Solomon had told certain individuals that Zara had disclosed a personal health matter to him. Zara states that the information was fabricated entirely.

When the rumor reached Zara, she confronted Solomon directly. Two witnesses describe the confrontation as controlled but intense. Solomon's reported response — "Prove I said it" — left the matter unresolved.

Zara raised the incident to a production liaison, who documented it without escalation. The rumor's potential for professional damage was significant: Zara's medical career and reputation in a conservative family context were both in jeopardy if the story spread beyond the show.

🔍 Documented Motive (as known to investigators) Defamation and professional harm. The false rumor attributed to Solomon could cause lasting damage to Zara's medical reputation. She had means (medical knowledge), motive (documented), and a known confrontation with the victim. However, she is also described as methodical and disciplined — investigators will need to weigh her behavioral profile carefully.
