# QUICKSTART GUIDE FOR GAME MASTERS
## Mystery 3A: "The Algorithm Knows"

This document serves as a one-page reference for running Mystery 3A.

### The Core Premise
Tech billionaire Solomon Vance has been electrocuted in his sensory deprivation infinity pool on the roof of a luxury resort in Cancún. 

### Gameplay Flow
1. **Briefing:** Players start at `mystery-3a/index.html`.
2. **Investigation:** They proceed to the Hub. They should review Suspect Dossiers.
3. **Evidence Tiers:**
   - **Tier 1 (Open):** Basic interviews and police reports. 
   - **Tier 2 (Locked):** Players must find the production Wi-Fi master password in Tier 1.
     - **UNLOCK CODE:** `FOREVER2025`
     - Promotes the investigation toward physical equipment logs.
   - **Tier 3 (Locked):** Players must find the system override phrase in Tier 2 to access the internal algorithm logs.
     - **UNLOCK CODE:** `EROS-AUDIT`
     - Delivers the decisive proof.
4. **Resolution:**
   - Players must construct a theory of Means, Motive, and Opportunity.
   - The GM verifies their theory or guides them back to the evidence.
   - Using the Solution Access Link, they reveal the truth.
     - **SOLUTION UNLOCK:** `VICTOR-SOTO-0847` 

### The Solution (Cheat Sheet)
- **Killer:** Victor Delgado (Real Name: Víctor Manuel Soto-Guerrero)
- **Motive:** Revenge. Vance's previous reality show drove Victor's sister to suicide in 2020.
- **Means:** Master IT access to wipe cameras. Slipped into Equip B1, took a 100ft extension cord and hair dryer, dropped it in the pool at 12:05 AM.
- **Proof:** T2-01 (Keycard Log at 11:47 PM), T3-01 (Partial Fingerprint on cord), T3-04 (Rodrigo saw him fleeing).
